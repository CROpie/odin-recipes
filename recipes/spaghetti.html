Bibimbap

A pretty easy to cook dish

The same recipe will work for most other pasta

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Jar of ready-made sauce
  • Bacon
  • Mushrooms
  • Basil

Cooking Guide

  1. Pasta
  2. Fry bacon and mushrooms
  3. Add sauce to pan
  4. Transfer pasta via tongs to pan
  5. Concentrate the sauce
