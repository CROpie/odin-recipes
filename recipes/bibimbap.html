Bibimbap

The key to this dish is gochujan sauce. Without it, it's just a stir-fry

For added flavour use a hot stone bowl

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Gochujan/sauce
  • Marinated beef
  • Whatever vegies you like *3
  • Mushrooms (eg shiitake)
  • Fried egg

Cooking Guide

  1. Cook rice
  2. Marinade & cook beef
  3. Cook vegies and mushrooms
  4. Fry egg
  5. Combine and eat
